CFPB Hack Day

Wednesday, January 27, 2016, 9 AM-4 PM

This event has been postponed until the week of April 11. Stay tuned for updates.


What is it?

A day of making things, including external resources for the public and internal tools - whether it's a website, an app, a gadget, or a physical object - to support the CFPB's mission and help government run more efficiently. There will be pizza.

Who's Invited?

All CFPB employees and contractors are invited to attend and propose projects for the event.

How can I participate?

Have a project idea? Propose it here.

Want to contribute your subject matter expertise, or design or code on a project? Join a project here.
